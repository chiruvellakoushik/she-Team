How TSWSW can help with cases of Street Sexual Harassment?

  • SHE Teams deal with the cases of Street Sexual Harassment. If you face or come across any case of Street Sexual Harassment you can reach out to SHE Teams for help. SHE Teams are available in different cities and towns across the state of Punjab to ensure the safety & security of women.

What to do if someone is harassing you in India?

  • If the complaint is not resolved at the workplace, the victim can approach the labour authorities or the police, depending on the nature and severity of the harassment.
  • In extreme mental harassment cases, victims can file a civil suit or criminal complaint as per the relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code
